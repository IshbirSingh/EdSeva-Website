Our Partners

Working together.

EdSeva works alongside university societies and orgs who share our belief in passing down knowledge and supporting the youth. Here’s who we’re working with.

Current Partners

Organisations we work with

Imperial Sikh Society

We are supported by the Sikh Society of Imperial College London, ranked by QS as the 2nd best University in the world. They support us through the provision of mentors and venues for our events.

Cambridge Sikh Society

Oxford Sikh Society

The Sikh Society of the University of Oxford, a household name known for academic excellence and reputation. They support us through the provision of mentors and venues for our events.

Why Partner With Us

What partners take away

Partnering with EdSeva creates a meaningful opportunity for partners to have a lasting impact by supporting and inspiring the next generation of young people, by engaging ambitious Sixth Formers with the opportunities and connections to succeed with their academic and career aspirations.

Additionally, EdSeva will provide partners with access to motivated university students who are passionate about mentoring and creating positive change. Our strong relationships with the societies of world-renowned institutions further creates valuable opportunities for collaboration.

  • Visibility with motivated sixth form students considering your university or field.
  • A ready-made outreach channel for events, talks, and admissions content.
  • A structured way to give back — we handle the logistics, your members give their support.

Interested in partnering?

We’re always open to working with new societies and organisations who share our goal. Please get in touch via email to discuss a potential partnership - we look forward to hearing from you!
