Rooted in seva, shaping futures for all.

Passing down experience and knowledge, from student to student.

Whether you need support for your personal statement, preparation for your admissions test, or help acing your interviews, EdSeva gives you access to free and openly accessible guidance from university mentors – the people whose firsthand experience and practical advice can help you achieve the same success and secure a place where they are today.

Our Mission

Sharing knowledge and experience between students.

Many students have high grades and potential, yet how many truly understand the admissions process? At EdSeva, we are here to bridge that gap and assist students in achieving the most that they can, through the passing down of knowledge from older students from top unis to younger ones aspiring to join them.

Though rooted in the Sikh value of Seva — selfless service — our support is open to every and any student that could benefit.

Online Events

Workshops, webinars, and interactive sessions, where you can learn and get direct feedback from mentors.

In Person Events

Networking made easy with in-person workshops and sessions to support your application, whilst connecting you with inspirational mentors.

Other Support

Get in touch with us at your convenience for some additional guidance and advice from your mentors.
